How to transfer your maps:
  1. In Inkarnate (above), create or open your map
  2. Click the "Export" button in Inkarnate
  3. Choose your preferred resolution (higher for better quality)
  4. Download the map as a PNG or JPG file
  5. In Owlbear Rodeo (below), click "Import" or drag the image into the scene
  6. Your map is now ready for gameplay!
